# Versioning Strategy

Stoxly follows **semantic versioning**.

Format:

MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

Example:

1.0.0

Definitions:

MAJOR
Breaking changes.

MINOR
New features.

PATCH
Bug fixes or small improvements.
